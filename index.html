What is Miku Marketing?

Hatsune Miku, officially code-named CV01, is a Vocaloid software voicebank developed by Crypton Future Media and its official anthropomorphic mascot character, a girl with long, turquoise twintails. Miku's personification has been marketed as a virtual idol, and has performed at live virtual concerts onstage as an animated projection. Hatsune Miku has inspired millions of people through the internet, let us do that for you.

  • What is Miku?

    Hatsune Miku is a virtual pop star created using Vocaloid technology, known for her iconic appearance with turquoise pigtails. She's an open-source character, allowing fans to create their own music and content with her voicebank. Miku has become a global cultural icon, with holographic concerts, collaborations, and a dedicated fanbase, making her a symbol of the virtual pop culture movement.
