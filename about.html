Who is Miku? Why Should She Market my Business?

Hatsune Miku is the logo of Japanese Vocaloid software CV-01. You may ask yourself, what is a vocaloid? A vocaloid is a program that turns text into singing. You can think of it like Siri, but she can sing! Hatsune Miku has experienced great success all over the globe. As a matter of fact, according to 2023 analytics data gather by Google, Hatsune Miku is more searched than Sonic the Hedgehog, and who doesn't know Sonic? Providing a familiar face for your business, gives you a leg up on the competition!

What is "Miku Marketing?"

Miku Marketing using familar faces to advertise products at a substantially lower cost than other popular celebrites. Studies show consumers are more likely to buy a product if it is recommended to them by someone they know, even if that person is someone they have never met. This is why you see celebrities always marketing products, and Hatsune Miku is no different. However, because Hatsune Miku is not a real person, the cost is just the cost of production! Get the superstar effect, without a superstar wallet.
